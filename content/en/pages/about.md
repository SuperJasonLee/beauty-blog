## About Beauty-Blog Medical Review Board

**Beauty-Blog.cloud-ip.cc** (AestheticWise / 美知医美) is a physician-reviewed aesthetic medicine knowledge platform dedicated to providing authoritative, objective, evidence-based aesthetic medicine information to a global audience. Founded in 2026, our platform bridges the gap between complex medical research and consumer understanding.

### Our Mission

In an era of information overload, consumers often struggle to distinguish reliable aesthetic medicine information from marketing hype and misinformation. According to ISAPS 2024 data, over 38 million aesthetic procedures were performed globally — yet many patients make decisions based on incomplete or inaccurate information. Our mission is:

- **Evidence-based content**: Every article cites authoritative medical journals (PubMed-indexed), professional organization data (ASPS, ISAPS, AAFPRS), and official regulatory communications (FDA, NMPA). We do not publish sponsored content or paid reviews.
- **Professional review**: All medical content undergoes a multi-stage review process by licensed physicians before publication. Each article displays the name of the reviewing physician and the date of last medical review.
- **Safety first**: We prioritize patient safety in every article. Surgical risks, complication rates, and contraindications are always disclosed — never minimized.
- **Information transparency**: We provide objective information without recommending specific institutions, practitioners, or commercial products. Our revenue comes solely from non-intrusive advertising.
- **Cross-cultural knowledge**: We publish in both English and Chinese to serve the global aesthetic medicine community, recognizing that the largest markets (US, China, Japan, South Korea, Thailand) each contribute unique innovations.

### What Makes Us Different

Unlike social media influencers and clinic-owned blogs, we maintain strict editorial independence. Our content pipeline works as follows:

1. **Topic selection**: Our editorial team identifies topics based on emerging trends (GLP-1 body contouring, regenerative aesthetics, AI in preoperative planning), regulatory changes (FDA safety communications, NMPA approvals), and persistent patient questions (recovery timelines, complication rates, revision surgery).
2. **Literature review**: For each article, editors conduct systematic searches across PubMed, Cochrane Library, and major society publications. We prioritize peer-reviewed studies, meta-analyses, and position statements from recognized professional bodies.
3. **Medical review**: Draft articles are sent to licensed physicians specializing in the relevant field. Reviewers verify clinical accuracy, assess risk disclosures, and confirm that recommendations align with current standards of care.
4. **Editorial polish**: Final articles are edited for clarity, readability, and completeness while preserving medical accuracy.
5. **Scheduled re-review**: Articles are flagged for re-review every 6 months to ensure ongoing accuracy as the field evolves.

### Our Content Review Process

1. **Initial draft**: Written by editors with medical backgrounds based on latest research and guidelines
2. **Professional review**: Submitted to licensed physicians for medical accuracy review
3. **Fact-checking**: All statistics and citations are verified against primary sources
4. **Regular updates**: Content is updated based on the latest medical advances

Our editorial board comprises:

- **Medical researchers**: Editors with advanced degrees in biomedical sciences who conduct literature reviews and synthesize clinical evidence

### Our Editorial Principles

1. **Accuracy over speed**: We prioritize verified information over breaking news. When uncertain, we wait for authoritative sources.
2. **Balance over sensationalism**: We present both benefits and risks of every procedure. No procedure is "risk-free" or "guaranteed."
3. **Education over persuasion**: Our goal is to inform, not to sell. We never pressure readers toward or away from any procedure.
4. **Inclusivity over exclusivity**: Aesthetic medicine serves people of all genders, ages, and backgrounds. Our content reflects this diversity.
