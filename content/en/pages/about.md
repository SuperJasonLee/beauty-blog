## About Beauty-Blog Medical Review Board

**Beauty-Blog.cloud-ip.cc** is a physician-reviewed aesthetic medicine knowledge platform dedicated to providing authoritative, objective, evidence-based aesthetic medicine information to a global audience.

### Our Mission

In an era of information overload, consumers often struggle to distinguish reliable aesthetic medicine information. Our mission is:

- **Evidence-based content**: All medical information cites authoritative medical journals and professional organization data
- **Professional review**: Content is reviewed by qualified healthcare professionals before publication
- **Safety first**: Help consumers understand surgical risks and make informed decisions
- **Information transparency**: Provide objective information without recommending specific institutions or practitioners

### Content Review Process

1. **Initial draft**: Written by editors with medical backgrounds based on latest research and guidelines
2. **Professional review**: Submitted to licensed physicians for medical accuracy review
3. **Fact-checking**: All statistics and citations are verified
4. **Regular updates**: Content is updated based on the latest medical advances

### Data Sources

We cite data from the following authoritative sources:

- **ISAPS** (International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery) — Global aesthetic surgery statistics

### Industry Data References

Based on the latest industry reports (2025-2026):

- **Global market**: Approximately 38 million aesthetic procedures performed worldwide in 2024, a 42.5% increase from four years prior (Source: ISAPS)
- **Non-surgical growth**: Non-invasive treatments account for 80% of all procedures in 2025, with neurotoxins and fillers as the most popular (Source: AAFPRS)
- **Male market**: 1.67 million male aesthetic procedures in the US in 2023, an 8% increase (Source: ASPS)

### Content Coverage

We cover the following aesthetic medicine areas:

- **Facial Surgery**: Rhinoplasty, blepharoplasty, facial contouring, jawline procedures
- **Skin Care**: Laser treatments, injectables (botulinum toxin, fillers), IPL
- **Body Contouring**: Liposuction, breast augmentation, abdominoplasty
- **Industry Trends**: Market dynamics, technological developments, policy regulations
