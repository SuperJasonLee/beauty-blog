### Professional Medical Advice

Before making any medical decisions, **always**:

1. Consult a healthcare professional with valid credentials and relevant specialization
2. Visit a licensed medical facility for a comprehensive evaluation and diagnosis
3. Fully understand the risks, benefits, alternatives, and expected outcomes of any procedure
4. Obtain a second opinion for any surgical intervention
5. Verify that all products and devices used are approved by the relevant regulatory authority (FDA, NMPA, CE, etc.)

### Risk Acknowledgment

Aesthetic surgical and non-surgical procedures carry risks including but not limited to:

- **General surgical risks**: Infection, bleeding, adverse reactions to anesthesia, blood clots
- **Procedure-specific risks**: Scarring, asymmetry, nerve damage, sensory changes, contour irregularities
- **Long-term considerations**: Implants may require future replacement; results may change with aging and weight fluctuation
- **Psychological impact**: Unrealistic expectations can lead to dissatisfaction even with technically successful outcomes

**All procedures carry risks. Please ensure you fully understand and accept the relevant risks before making a decision about any aesthetic intervention.**
