Before making any medical decisions, **always**:

1. Consult a healthcare professional with valid credentials
2. Visit a licensed medical facility for a comprehensive evaluation
3. Fully understand the risks, benefits, and alternatives of any procedure
4. Obtain a second opinion if needed

### Risk Notice

Aesthetic surgical procedures carry risks including but not limited to:

- General surgical risks such as infection, bleeding, and anesthesia reactions
- Results that may not meet expectations
- Post-operative complications such as scarring and hyperpigmentation
- The possibility of requiring revision surgery

**All surgical procedures carry risks. Please ensure you fully understand and accept the relevant risks before making a decision about surgery.**
