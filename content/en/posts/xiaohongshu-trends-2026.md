## 2026 Xiaohongshu Aesthetic Medicine Landscape

Xiaohongshu (小红书/XHS), China's largest lifestyle sharing platform, publishes monthly aesthetic medicine industry reports that have become authoritative indicators of market trends. This article compiles data from 2025-2026 official XHS reports to present the most noteworthy aesthetic medicine trends.

---

## 🔥 Top Trending Procedures

### 1. IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) — The Evergreen Champion

![IPL photorejuvenation treatment in a modern aesthetic clinic](/images/posts/en-xiaohongshu-trends-2026/ipl_treatment.jpg)

Heat Index: 702K+

IPL photon rejuvenation remains the most popular aesthetic medicine topic on Xiaohongshu, topping charts for months. The search query "Which is more worth it — mesotherapy or IPL?" grew 30.52% year-over-year, indicating consumers are comparing options before deciding.

### 2. Gold Microneedling — #1 Procedure Search

![Gold microneedling RF device with fractional tip](/images/posts/en-xiaohongshu-trends-2026/gold_microneedling.jpg)

Heat Index: 1.87M

Gold microneedling has surged to the #1 procedure search term. Users are most interested in "before/after comparison images" and "5th generation technology." Searches for "gold microneedling efficacy and side effects" are also rising, showing consumers care about both results and safety.

### 3. Facial Contouring — Dominating Social Topics

![Facial contouring dermal filler injection procedure](/images/posts/en-xiaohongshu-trends-2026/facial_contouring.jpg)

Topic Views: 1.65 Billion

"Facial contouring fixation" (轮廓固定) leads aesthetic medicine social topics by a wide margin, with derivative terms occupying 4 of the top 10 spots. Consumers now追求整体面部的紧致与流畅, shifting from isolated procedures to holistic facial harmony.

### 4. Mesotherapy & Collagen — The Steady Foundation

Mesotherapy (水光针) and collagen fillers form the foundation of aesthetic medicine. While competition is intense, traffic acquisition costs remain low. Brand-associated keywords like "Hi Body" and "Biohyalux" are gaining search traction, reflecting a shift from procedure-focused to brand-focused searches.

---

## 📈 Fastest Growing Dark Horse Procedures

### Glycation Face — 930% Surge

"Glycation face" (糖化脸) saw an explosive 930% month-over-month increase. Increased social gatherings, late-night work, and higher sugar intake lead to dull, yellowed, and sagging skin. "Anti-glycation" and "anti-oxidation" have become skincare keywords.

### Tear Trough — 113.5% Surge

Searches for "how to fix tear trough depression" surged 113.5%. Anti-aging demand is penetrating to precise periorbital areas. Mid-face, periorbital, and lip rejuvenation needs are growing rapidly.

### Nasal Base Depression — 28.56% Growth

"How to improve nasal base depression" searches continue rising, with "nose injections" growing 32.73%. Consumers increasingly understand the role of nasal base volume in overall facial dimension.

---

## ⚠️ Emerging Pain Point: Overfill Repair

![Overfill repair treatment with hyaluronidase filler dissolution](/images/posts/en-xiaohongshu-trends-2026/overfill_repair.jpg)

"Overfill repair," "overfilled face," and "post-overfill syndrome" — all three related terms entered the search TOP 20. Years of excessive filler use have created a backlash, with consumers seeking "regret remedies." Clinics that position around "repair," "natural look," and "anti-fake face" will capture this high-value demographic.

---

## 💉 Minimally Invasive vs. Surgical Trends

Category breakdown:
- **Skin aesthetics (minimally invasive)**: 78.93% — dominant traffic driver
- **Facial contouring (surgical)**: 21.07% — significant growth potential

Minimally invasive procedures (injectables, energy-based devices) continue dominating. However, surgical searches account for 34.47% of aesthetic medicine queries, showing that surgical demand remains strong. Consumers increasingly follow a "research surgery first, try minimally invasive later" decision path.

## 🔮 2026 H2 Trend Predictions

1. **Regenerative materials on the rise**: Sculptra, Radiesse, and other collagen-stimulating treatments will maintain high growth
2. **Combination therapies become standard**: Single procedures are no longer sufficient — "mesotherapy + collagen," "botox + fillers" combinations are trending
3. **Overfill repair demand persists**: The aftermath of over-filling will continue driving the repair market
4. **Emotional value drives consumption**: XHS's "2026 Beauty Emotion Insight Whitepaper" incorporates "emotional aesthetics" into purchase decisions
5. **Accelerated compliance**: Tighter regulation; compliant accounts saw 35% average exposure increase

## Frequently Asked Questions

{{< faq >}}
- **What is the dominant 2026 consumer trend on Xiaohongshu?** Public coverage consistently describes a shift toward research-driven, qualification-conscious consumers, with rising interest in combination therapies and regenerative treatments[^1][^2][^3].
- **Are "men's medical aesthetic" procedures a real growth segment?** The trend is reported in multiple Chinese-language industry reports[^1][^2] — exact percentage contributions vary across reports and should be treated as qualitative directional indicators rather than precise figures.
- **Is RF microneedling (gold microneedling) safe?** The FDA issued a safety communication in October 2025 about certain RF microneedling uses, citing reports of burns, scarring, fat loss, and nerve damage[^4]. Discuss parameters with a licensed practitioner before treatment.
- **What should I look for in a qualified aesthetic provider?** Beijing Chaoyang's "Compliance Guidelines for Medical Aesthetic Advertising"[^2] and SAMR livestream-e-commerce rules[^3] both emphasize verifying the facility's license, the practitioner's credentials, and the product's authenticity.
- **What is the 2026 outlook for combination therapies?** Public industry reports describe combination protocols (e.g., skin-boosters + collagen stimulators; botulinum toxin + hyaluronic acid) as a growing direction, with single-modality treatments less able to meet consumer expectations[^1][^2][^3].
