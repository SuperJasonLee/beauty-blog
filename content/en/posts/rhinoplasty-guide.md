The nose is the central feature of the face and plays a crucial role in overall facial aesthetics.

## Overview of Rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty is a surgical procedure that changes the shape and size of the nose. It can be classified by purpose:

- **Cosmetic Rhinoplasty**: Improves nasal appearance
- **Functional Rhinoplasty**: Improves nasal breathing
- **Revision Rhinoplasty**: Corrects congenital deformities or post-traumatic defects

## Common Implant Materials

| Material | Advantages | Disadvantages | Duration |
|----------|------------|---------------|----------|
| Silicone Implant | Stable, easy to remove | May shift or show translucency | Long-term |
| ePTFE (Gore-Tex) | Good tissue compatibility, no shifting | Difficult to remove | Long-term |
| Autologous Cartilage (Septal/Ear) | No rejection risk | Limited supply, may resorb | Long-term |

## Pre-operative Assessment

- Facial proportion analysis (nasofrontal angle, nasolabial angle, etc.)
- Skin thickness evaluation (affects surgical outcome)
- Internal nasal structure examination
- General health assessment

Choosing an experienced plastic surgeon is the key to a successful rhinoplasty. Review the surgeon's before-and-after photos to determine if their aesthetic style matches your expectations.
{{< /alert >}}

## Post-operative Care

1. **Head elevation**: Keep head elevated for 48 hours to reduce swelling
2. **Cold compress**: Apply intermittently for the first 72 hours
3. **Avoid impact**: Avoid any contact with the nose for one month
4. **No strenuous exercise**: Avoid for one month
5. **Glasses**: Avoid resting glasses on the nasal bridge for at least 4 weeks

{{< term "Nasal splinting" >}}Medical tape and a splint are used to immobilize the nose after surgery, protecting the surgical site and maintaining the new shape{{< /term >}}

## Expected Recovery Timeline

- **1-2 weeks**: Splint and sutures removed, most swelling subsides
- **3-4 weeks**: Can resume most daily activities
- **3-6 months**: Nasal shape largely stabilized
- **12 months**: Final result fully apparent
