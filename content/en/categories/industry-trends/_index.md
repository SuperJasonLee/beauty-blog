## Industry Trends

Deep-dive analysis of emerging trends in aesthetic medicine consumer behavior and market dynamics. We track what patients are searching for, what procedures are gaining popularity, and how consumer preferences are evolving.

- **Procedure Popularity**: Rising and declining procedures, regional variations
- **Market Segmentation**: Gender differences, age group trends, geographic patterns
- **Technology Impact**: AI in preoperative planning, telemedicine, digital consultations
