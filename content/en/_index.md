## Welcome to AestheticWise

**AestheticWise (美知医美)** is a physician-reviewed aesthetic medicine knowledge platform providing authoritative, objective, and evidence-based information to help you make informed decisions about aesthetic procedures.

Our editorial team — consisting of licensed physicians, medical researchers, and healthcare journalists — produces in-depth articles that cite peer-reviewed journals, official regulatory communications, and data from leading professional organizations including ASPS, ISAPS, and AAFPRS.

## Why Trust Our Content?

Every article on AestheticWise goes through a rigorous editorial process:

1. **Systematic Literature Review**: Editors search PubMed, Cochrane Library, and major society publications for the latest evidence
2. **Physician Medical Review**: Licensed physicians specializing in the relevant field verify clinical accuracy and risk disclosures
3. **Fact-Checking**: All statistics, procedure counts, and regulatory information are verified against primary sources
4. **Scheduled Re-Review**: Articles are flagged for re-evaluation every 6 months to ensure ongoing accuracy

## Our Commitment to Safety

Patient safety is at the heart of everything we publish. Every article includes:

- Clear risk disclosures and complication rates where available
- Guidance on verifying practitioner credentials and facility licensing
- Evidence-based recovery timelines and aftercare recommendations
- Unbiased comparisons of different approaches and technologies

**We do not accept payment for positive reviews, recommend specific clinics or practitioners, or publish sponsored content.** Our advertising comes exclusively from non-intrusive Google AdSense placements that support our mission of free, high-quality medical education.
