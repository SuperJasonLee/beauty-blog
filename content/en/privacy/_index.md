## Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

### Introduction

Aesthetic Medicine Knowledge Base (also known as AestheticWise / 美知医美, "this Website", "we", "us") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information when you visit our website at beauty-blog.cloud-ip.cc and its subdomains.

This Privacy Policy applies to users located in any jurisdiction, including the European Economic Area (EEA), California, and China. Where applicable, we comply with GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and China's Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL).

By accessing or using this Website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### Information We Collect

#### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this Website, our servers and analytics providers automatically collect the following technical information:

- **Log Data**: Your IP address (anonymized), browser type and version, operating system, device type, access date and time, referring URL, pages viewed, time spent on each page, and clickstream data
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies**: We use cookies, web beacons, local storage, and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage patterns, and serve relevant content

#### Information You Provide Voluntarily

- **Contact Information**: When you contact us via email (editor@beauty-blog.cloud-ip.cc), we collect your name, email address, and message content
- **Newsletter Subscriptions**: If you subscribe to any newsletter or mailing list, we collect your email address and preferences
- **Search Queries**: When you use our search function, the queries you enter are logged temporarily for functionality purposes

#### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect sensitive personal data including but not limited to:
- Health or medical information
- Biometric data
- Racial or ethnic origin
- Political opinions
- Religious beliefs
- Genetic data

### How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following legitimate purposes:

- **Website Operation**: Providing, maintaining, and improving the functionality of this Website
- **User Experience**: Personalizing content delivery, remembering preferences, and ensuring proper display across devices
- **Analytics**: Understanding visitor patterns and traffic sources to improve content relevance and site structure
- **Communication**: Responding to your inquiries, feedback, or support requests
- **Legal Compliance**: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
- **Security**: Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues, fraud, or security threats

### Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, our legal bases for processing your personal data are:

- **Contractual necessity**: To provide the Website and its features
- **Legitimate interests**: To improve our content, analyze usage, and ensure security
- **Consent**: Where you have explicitly consented (e.g., non-essential cookies)

You have the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on prior consent.

**Important**: Google AdSense does not use your personally identifiable information (such as your name or email) to serve personalized ads. Ad targeting is based on aggregated, anonymized browsing behavior.

This Website uses Google Analytics (UA or GA4) to analyze traffic patterns. Google Analytics collects anonymized visit data, including:

Learn more about Google's privacy practices: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Google Analytics Privacy](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/terms/us/).

This Website uses content delivery networks (CDNs) and hosting providers to ensure fast global access. These services may temporarily cache static assets and log basic access information.

### Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information:

- **TLS/SSL encryption**: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted
- **Regular security assessments**: We perform routine vulnerability scans and security audits
- **Access controls**: Personal data is accessible only to authorized personnel
- **Incident response**: We have procedures in place to detect, respond to, and notify affected individuals of data breaches as required by applicable law

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:
