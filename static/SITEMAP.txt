# AestheticWise Knowledge Base

Welcome to AestheticWise (美知医美) — your trusted source for physician-reviewed aesthetic medicine information.

## What We Cover

Our editorial team provides comprehensive, evidence-based articles on:

- **Facial Surgery**: Rhinoplasty, blepharoplasty, facelift techniques, facial contouring
- **Breast Surgery**: Augmentation, lift, reduction, revision, fat transfer
- **Body Contouring**: Liposuction, abdominoplasty, post-weight-loss procedures
- **Injectables & Skin**: Botox, dermal fillers, laser treatments, RF microneedling
- **Hair Restoration**: Transplantation techniques, medical therapies
- **Industry News**: Regulatory updates, market trends, technology innovations

## Our Promise

Every article is reviewed by licensed physicians and cites authoritative sources including PubMed-indexed journals, ASPS, ISAPS, and FDA communications. We do not publish sponsored content or accept payment for favorable coverage.
